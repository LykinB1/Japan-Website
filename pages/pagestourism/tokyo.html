TOKYO

Tokyo is the capital of Japan and the seat of the national government.


It is considered one of the largest metropolises on the planet and also the political, economic, educational, and cultural center of Japan.


The metropolis represents one of the world's largest urban agglomerations. The metropolis is constituted by 23 wards, 26 additional municipalities, and the Izu and Ogasawara islands.

Main Attraction

Meiji Temple

Surrounded by lush green forest right in the heart of Tokyo, this Shinto shrine is dedicated to Emperor Meiji (1852-1912) and Empress Shoken, who led Japan into becoming a modern nation.


Meiji Jingu should be a priority among your first tours in Tokyo, as any visit to the bustling Shibuya or Harajuku areas pairs well with a peaceful walk along the well-marked paths to the main shrine.
