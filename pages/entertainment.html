MANGÁS

It's almost impossible to speak about making an Anime without speaking about Mangas first.

The Mangás are one form of literature so much known in Japan and in many places around the world.

Just like American comics, Mangas are comics with various types of art and they tell stories similar to other types of comics.

They are normally known for the amazing and (sometimes) insane or strange art.
